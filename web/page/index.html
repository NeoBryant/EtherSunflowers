Instructions:

1. Please choose your account first.

2. Make sure you have enough suns

    When you don't have any suns, you can click "getFreeSuns" to get 10 suns, which you can check it out by getSunsBalance.

    You can buy suns with your ETH. (1 ETH can buy 1 sun)

3. Click getSunflowersBySuns, 1 sunflower requires 10 suns.

4. Now you have sunflowers, click "work", you will get 20*(sunflowersNum) suns, and the sunflowers are gone.

5. If you quit, then failAmount++. If you rest, then successAmount++.

6. You can sell all your suns by clicking "sellSuns" if the contract have enough ETH.

You can donate to the contract. Have fun!
